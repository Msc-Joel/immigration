For years, US has been home for sportsmen and women

By Joel Konopo

June 20, 2022


Athletes nationally or even internationally recognized for their sporting achievements are mostly eligible for a non-immigrant US visa called O-1 visa. This visa has an initial limit of 3 years and can be renewed for another 1 year. Over the years, professionals have been relocating to the US taking advantage of advanced training and signing opportunities. This has led to an increase in the number of profesisonal atheletes relocating to the US. Data shows that athlets from countries such as Mexico and Germany have enjoyed greater opportunities in the US with their spouses qualifying for an O-3 visa, to also remain in the USA for a specified time.
